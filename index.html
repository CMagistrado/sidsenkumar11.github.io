My Story

Hi there! Thanks for visiting my page.

I'm a 2nd year CS major studying at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I've decided to focus my education on theory and system architecture to learn about computer security.

I'm fascinated by information security and cryptography. With proven encryption standards being enforced, why are health insurers, banks, and even the U.S. Government being so easily and successfully attacked? What can we do to prevent these issues and ensure secure, reliable information management?

To answer these questions, I am pursuing a degree in computer science and am delving into the precise mathematical techniques we use to hide our data as well as the technology that supports them. Eventually, my goal is to apply my knowledge to create both safe and practical solutions to an organization's information security needs.
