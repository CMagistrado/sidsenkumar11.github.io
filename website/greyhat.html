Meeting 1 Problems

This first week's problem set will be about cryptography. I'll be dividing the problems up into 3-4 categories of difficulty to give people of all levels a chance to learn during these meetings.

Some of you may be unfamiliar with cryptography. Put simply, it's the art of writing codes to disguise or hide information from others. On the flip side, we have something called cryptanalysis. Cryptanalysis is the art of breaking and deciphering codes. Today we'll practice learning about some of the most famous (and well studied) cryptographic systems available, and how to break them. For most of the problems, I will give you a text and I'd like for you to decrypt this text. If you think you've got the plaintext (readable text) or have any questions, feel free to ask me and I'll do my best to help you.
